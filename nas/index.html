NAS & Local Cloud

Your own private "cloud" at home.

Stop paying monthly fees for cloud storage. A Network Attached Storage (NAS) device gives you central storage for photos, videos, documents, and backups—all under your control.

Central storage for photos, videos, and documents

A NAS is like having your own Dropbox or Google Drive, but it lives in your home. Instead of storing files on individual devices or paying for cloud storage, everything goes to one central location that every device on your network can access.

On-site backup and optional off-site backup

A NAS is perfect for backing up your computers automatically. We'll configure Time Machine for Macs and Windows Backup for PCs, so your computers are always backed up without you thinking about it. For extra protection, we can also set up automatic off-site backups to services like Backblaze B2.

Secure remote access while traveling

Just because your NAS is at home doesn't mean you can't access it from anywhere. We'll set up secure remote access via VPN or a secure tunnel, so you can access your files, photos, and backups from your phone or laptop while traveling.

Optional media server (Plex, etc.)

Many NAS devices are powerful enough to run media servers like Plex, Jellyfin, or Emby. That means you can store your movie and music collections on the NAS and stream them to any device in your home—or remotely.

Good fit if you:

  • Have lots of photos, videos, or documents to store.
  • Want to stop paying for cloud storage subscriptions.
  • Need automatic backups for multiple computers.
  • Want to access your files from anywhere securely.
