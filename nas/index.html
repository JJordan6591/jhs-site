NAS & Local Cloud

Your own private "cloud" at home.

Stop paying monthly fees for cloud storage. A Network Attached Storage (NAS) device gives you central storage for photos, videos, documents, and backups—all under your control, accessible from any device, and optionally backed up off-site.

Central storage for photos, videos, and documents

A NAS is like having your own Dropbox or Google Drive, but it lives in your home. Instead of storing files on individual devices or paying for cloud storage, everything goes to one central location that every device on your network can access.

We'll set up shared folders for photos, documents, videos, and whatever else you need. You can access them from your laptop, phone, tablet, or smart TV. No more emailing files to yourself or plugging in USB drives.

On-site backup and optional off-site / cloud backup

A NAS is perfect for backing up your computers automatically. We'll configure Time Machine for Macs and Windows Backup for PCs, so your computers are always backed up without you thinking about it.

For extra protection, we can also set up automatic off-site backups. Your NAS can sync important folders to services like Backblaze B2 or AWS S3, or to another NAS at a different location. That way, even if something happens to your home, your data is safe.

Secure remote access to your NAS while traveling

Just because your NAS is at home doesn't mean you can't access it from anywhere. We'll set up secure remote access via VPN or a secure tunnel, so you can access your files, photos, and backups from your phone or laptop while traveling.

Unlike cloud services, you control the security. We'll configure strong encryption, two-factor authentication, and proper firewall rules so only you can access your data.

Optional media server (Plex, etc.)

Many NAS devices are powerful enough to run media servers like Plex, Jellyfin, or Emby. That means you can store your movie and music collections on the NAS and stream them to any device in your home—or remotely, if you set it up.

We can set up a media server as part of your NAS installation, or add it later if you want to start with just storage and backups.

Integration with your network

A NAS works best when it's part of a well-designed network. We'll connect it via Ethernet for maximum speed, put it on a secure VLAN if needed, and configure your network so all your devices can access it easily.

If you're getting a full network upgrade, we can include NAS setup from the start. If you already have a network, we'll integrate the NAS with what you have.

Good fit if you:

  • Have lots of photos, videos, or documents to store.
  • Want to stop paying for cloud storage subscriptions.
  • Need automatic backups for multiple computers.
  • Want to access your files from anywhere securely.
  • Want a media server for movies and music.
