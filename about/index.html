About

Local, reliable, privacy-focused home tech.

Jordan Home Solutions designs and installs networks and smart homes you'd want in your own house. Based in Cincinnati, serving the greater area with honest recommendations and clean installs.

Who I am

I'm Jordan, and I'm local to the Cincinnati area—specifically Anderson and the surrounding communities. I started Jordan Home Solutions because I got tired of seeing people overpay for mediocre networking solutions or struggle with systems that break when the internet goes down.

My background is in IT and networking, but I've always been interested in making technology work better for regular people. That means designing systems that are powerful enough to handle real-world usage but simple enough that you don't need to become a networking expert to use them.

My philosophy

I build networks and smart homes I'd want in my own house. That means:

  • Reliability first: Systems that work consistently, not just when everything is perfect.
  • Privacy by design: Local-first solutions that keep your data in your home.
  • Clean installs: Professional wiring, proper placement, and attention to detail.
  • Honest recommendations: I'll tell you if a simpler solution is all you need, or if you're better off with something more robust.
  • No upsell pressure: You get what you need, not what I can sell you.

What I do

I design and install:

  • Whole-home UniFi WiFi networks for homes that need reliable coverage
  • Home NAS storage systems for central storage and backups
  • Security camera systems with local recording
  • Smart home automation using local-first platforms like Home Assistant
  • VPN and remote access setup for secure access to your home network

Every project is custom-designed for your home and your needs. I don't use cookie-cutter solutions or one-size-fits-all packages. We'll start with a free assessment, figure out what you actually need, and build from there.

Areas I serve

I primarily serve the greater Cincinnati area, including Anderson, Indian Hill, and surrounding communities. If you're not sure if I serve your area, just ask—I'm happy to discuss whether I can help with your project.

Experience & projects

I've worked on projects ranging from small apartment WiFi tune-ups to large multi-story homes with dozens of devices, cameras, and smart home integrations. Every project teaches me something new, and I bring that experience to each new installation.

Types of projects I've completed:

  • UniFi network installations in homes from 1,500 to 5,000+ sq ft
  • NAS setups for photo storage, backups, and media servers
  • Camera systems with local recording and remote access
  • Home Assistant smart home automation setups
  • Network troubleshooting and optimization for existing setups
