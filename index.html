How it works

Simple, transparent install process

No jargon, no surprise charges. Just a clear plan for fixing your WiFi and home network the right way.

  • 1
    Free in-home WiFi assessment
    We walk your home, measure signal strength, and review your current ISP and router. You get a simple summary of what's slowing you down.
  • 2
    Custom UniFi & NAS plan
    Based on your home layout and devices, you'll receive a clear proposal showing recommended hardware, estimated speeds, and pricing.
  • 3
    Install, configure, verify
    Hardware is installed, networks and VLANs are configured, and coverage is tested room-by-room before we call it done.
  • 4
    Handoff & support
    You'll get an easy-to-read network map, WiFi QR codes, and clear instructions for VPN or NAS access. Optional ongoing tune-ups are available.
